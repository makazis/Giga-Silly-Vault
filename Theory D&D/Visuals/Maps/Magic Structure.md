# Magic Structure Text
So, as previously mentioned, there are three subdivisions of [[Magic]]: [[Soul]] Manipulation, [[Pattern Manipulation]], and [[Alien]]s/[[Science]]. 

This means that All existing magic can be divided into one of the three different ways of magic. 

They should also get catchier names, so how about this for the names: 
[[Alien]]s/Science: "Astral"
[[Soul]] Manipulation: "Lisal"
[[Pattern Manipulation]]: "Fractal"

How about we straight up make the runes for pattern manipulators only? That being the way they get to access their magic system. Sounds pretty darn cool imo.
