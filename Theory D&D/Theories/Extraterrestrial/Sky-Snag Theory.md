#Theory 
[[UFO]]s are a result of a chemical reaction in the air. This means UFO's aren't real, and can also be induced by [[The Government]]. They can do that by manipulating the weather, and they will have the perfect shape and everything else. 

This of course isn't true for all the [[UFO]]s, because how else the [[Alien]]s would be able to contact the earth? No, this is a manipulation tactic by the goverment, in order to either lure out people who are aligned with aliens, or to temporarily grant their own soldiers powers beyond what is deemed possible.
