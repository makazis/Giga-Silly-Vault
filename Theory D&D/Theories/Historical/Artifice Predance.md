# Explanation:

Relates to [[The Great Cycle]], that has been mentioned a few times up until now.  (Look into that later)

In all of human history, there seem to be these pieces of artificial construction that have shown up before we started using them. They have aided the human revolution which is weird that they have also been there before we started using them. It is also the idea that things like aqueducts and [[The Pyramids]] were discovered, forgotten, and then rediscovered. From the last cycle we have these artifacts that have been left for us from the previous people.

# Interpretation:
Some of the [[Magic]]al artifacts were perhaps left behind by the humans from the last cycle. This would explain how we’d have artifacts that nobody knows how to produce or replicate. [[Artifact]]s that have survived the passage of time through either supernatural construction, or [[Time Capsules]].
