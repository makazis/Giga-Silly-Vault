# Explanation
The [[Templars Order]], also known as the Knights Templar, were the crusaders put together by [[The Catholic Church]] during the crusades. 

The Templars order invented more things in history than history gives them credit for. They still exist to this day, and are the spiritual descendants of the masons that were created and founded by [[Solomon]] during the construction of the temple in Jerusalem. King Solomon, who was the son of [[King David]], put together the masons in order to build the temple, and keep whatever secrets he had, close. And then over the years, the same group maintained these secrets within themselves, and eventually became the Templars Order. 

The [[Alien]]s helped the Egyptians. Some of the signage and the symbolism around the pyramids is very similar to that used by the masons, and therefore, the templars. The masons were created to guard safely kept knowledge. The Templars Order had advanced knowledge of what the world was like before everything reset itself. So, through history, the masons have all the information, all the knowledge from the world before. And they are now enacting that in our current iteration. 

The pyramid built is so advanced, because the builders had knowledge of the previous cycle. It’s also because they knew how it was built on the first go around.
