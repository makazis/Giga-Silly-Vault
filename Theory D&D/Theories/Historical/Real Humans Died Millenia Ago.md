#Theory 
# Explanation:

There used to be a higher form of [[Human]]s millennia ago. [[The Pyramids]] and [[Atlantis]] were built by these prime humans. The humans of this age aren’t the same humans as back then, and we are a lot weaker because of that. The previous humans could have been anything, [[Reptile People]], mermaids, elves, giants, nobody knows what exactly.

# Interpretation:
There is enough inbreeding between the different original fantasy races that creates the diversity and the unity in modern humans, giving us all the things that we now are. Intellect from the [[Elf]]s, ability to swim from the [[Mermaid]]s, ability to make cool things from the [[Gnome]]s and the [[Giant]]s, etc.
