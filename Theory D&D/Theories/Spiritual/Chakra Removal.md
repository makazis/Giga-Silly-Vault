#Theory #Concept #Action
This is the idea that a [[Human]] can take a [[Chakra]], and remove it from the [[Body]]. Chakras are the different windows for the [[Soul]] and different places of Energy (like [[Mana]]) within the body. So by removing a [[Chakra]] it would be kind of like [[Theory D&D/Things/Magic/Misc/Forced Soul Removal|Forced Soul Removal]], or a piece of soul removed. You can do this to deprive someone of their humanity, and to weaken their essence, turning them partially into a [[Soulless]] one. 
This has happened during the [[MK Ultra]] Experiments. This is also when [[The Government]] tried to produce [[Sleeper Agent]]s by removing the Chakra from the body and reinserting it with a code in order to activate the individual in order to have a mindless agent ([[Soulless]]).
