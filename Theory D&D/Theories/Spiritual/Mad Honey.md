#Theory 
This is about a special kind of honey, obtained in Nepal, Turkey, where bees get the pollen from rhonodendron flowers. This creates a honey that contains a neurotoxin, and has hallucinogenic effects. This allows you to access higher order thoughts, [[Hyper Reality]], and basically all of [[Fractal]] [[Magic]]. 
Several monks believe that this honey allows you to interact with the [[Spiritual Plane]]. 
Historically used by armies to poison other armies, cause they taught the honey was just honey. In large enough amounts can kill you too.
