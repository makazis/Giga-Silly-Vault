# **Explanation:**
A predecessor to the despair code. Traced all the way back to old cold war russian documents. Documents that were researching memetic entities. 

There’s a theory around, about shared consciousness and shared stories. Someone on one side of the planet can develop ideas spontaneously as someone on the other side of the planet does as well. This exists in a cloud that encompasses everyone at once. And if it exists like a shared memory bank, can you transmit a virus through it?

Virus 23 is a self replicating mental reagent. Comes from one person, is uploaded to the cloud of understanding, and is passed down the line.

Causes paranoia, depression, bad mentality, etc. 

Could literally be anything that triggers this. 

Subtheories:

- Started by Cicada, the people who hosted the nation wide scavenger hunt. 
    
- Hearing the words “Virus 23” is enough to transmit the virus. 
    
- Can be hidden in an unassuming thing. Stories, folklore, other stuff, etc.
