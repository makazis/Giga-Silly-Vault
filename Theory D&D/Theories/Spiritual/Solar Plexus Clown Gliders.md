People have seen faces after getting this demon, and it is like a curse that can be passed around online, and was in a chain email in the 1990s. This is a glitch in [[The Simulation]], and whoever finds a way to exploit it can cause it to get out. And whatever solar plexus clown glider is, it did get out.
