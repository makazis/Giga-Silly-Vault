#Theory 
# **Explanation:**

Some people will not be granted ascension into the next plane of existence. If souls exist as these eternal entities, and things such as reincarnation exist, then some can remember past instances, and can remember the world before the shift. These people with higher understanding of the world than the rest of us can have grudges against the people here on earth. 

Ascension blacklist states that the [[Illuminati]], or other cults, like [[The Government]] can decide who will, and who will not ascend beyond.

Subtheories:

- The [[Soulless]] ones will not be able to go beyond, as their soul won’t be able to leave their [[Corpse]], due to them having no soul. The soulless ones are also set up as a test for the ones with the souls to see if they are worthy to ascend to the beyond. Some of the soulless ones can even bring devoted enough people back on the whitelist.

# **Interpretation:**
Some people have say on whether or not other people get to go to heaven or to hell. This means that those people are likely to form cults, or secret societies, or be recruited by such societies at times.

### Connection with previous theories:

- [[Incorruptible Saints]]: if a [[Soul]] refuses to exit a body, and they are sentenced to heaven, that is the requirement to become an incorruptible saint. This means that the people who are registered to become such saints are usually kept within the hideouts of the cults, either having a death sentence, after which their soul is ripped from their body and they are finally put to rest, or they agree to guard the cult from outside forces, meaning they are forever on site, as an undead defense against the evil.
