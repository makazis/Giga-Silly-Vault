# **Explanation:**
There’s a finite amount of [[Soul]]s in [[The Universe]], which are from a pool known as the [[chamber of Guf]], that exists at the base of [[The Tree Of Life]]. The tree bears fruit, and the fruit are the [[Human]] souls. When a child is born, [[Gabriel]] reaches into the chamber, near the base of the tree, where the fruit has fallen, grabs a random soul fruit, and places it into the newborn person. 

The tree has seasons of plenty, and seasons of famine, which means there are sometimes more people in [[Human Realm|the world]] that the tree can provide. If someone is born at a time where no soul can be found for the individual, the person will be born [[Soulless|without a soul]]. In the current day, at the highest the population has ever been, the tree cannot sustain all the people in the world. The people without souls are known as NPCs, who exist in the world along with the people with the souls in them, and are in some cases used to test those who do have souls, to judge their worth. 

So there are some people that do not have control of their own actions, so they are purposefully made to judge those that have souls and can be judged. This means that the people who disagree with you are a deliberate choice by the creators of the system. 

Several cults or satanic rings who perform human sacrifices are sending a soul back to the tree for replenishing. 
    
When the tree runs out of souls completely, that is the apocalypse, and the world will end.
# **Interpretation:**

There’s a limited number of souls in the world. A number that grows smaller and smaller in the population each year, due to the rising number of people in the world. This means that there are some people born without souls. 

If there are no theories as to how to create a soul, the amount of souls will not replenish, and we’ll have to work in a system with few souls. 

When a person is born without a soul, they will not have free will or be able to act for themselves.

There are cults that replenish the tree by sacrificing people, to prevent the end of the world, because when the souls run out, the end of the world will come.
