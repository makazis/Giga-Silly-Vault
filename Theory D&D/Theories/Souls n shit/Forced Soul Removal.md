#Theory 
# Explanation:
A [[Soul]] can be taken from you, through cult rituals or disbelief, or traumatic events.

Traumatic events and disbelief can take the soul away by little pieces at a time. This leaves a person open to an attack where other souls can fill that space inside a person, and that’s where things like [[Possession]] come from. 

Wayward souls disconnected from [[Human]]s float around, and in large enough quantities can gain forms such as those of a [[Ghost]] or a [[Cryptid]]. Old asylums are haunted, because of all the souls floating around there. Wayward souls can also create Ambient [[Mana]] for spellcasting. 

The way to prevent this is positive aura, and a positive environment around yourself, because if you stop believing, and if you lose focus of yourself, you leave yourself vulnerable.

# Interpretation:
With enough sadness, your [[Soul]] dies away with your body, causing your body to be more vulnerable to negative energy, and other soul based attacks. This does mean that there is a whole secret society that can turn you into a [[Soulless]], or even worse, that souls can possess other bodies given enough power or the other soul being sad enough. This enables things like soul swapping, soul removal, etc. 

The theory also explains that cults can remove souls from bodies, and hints at the theory that such societies can push a soul into a body, thereby either possessing it, or controlling it. There’s a lot to unpack from this one, but it does allow for some really cool stuff to be done.
