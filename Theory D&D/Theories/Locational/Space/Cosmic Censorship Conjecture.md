#Theory 
The idea about singularities in physics, where things approach infinity, and something really weird happens. 
This is because if you reach a theorethical infinite in physics, you can do really wonky stuff. Things like starting a black hole or breaking a speed of light are hard to do because of this. 

There's a weird veil put over stuff in physics when this weird stuff happens, like the event horizon of a black hole. 

Whenever something is allowed to break the limits of [[The Universe]], we aren't allowed to watch it. This is one of the limits of the CPU that manages [[The Simulation]].
