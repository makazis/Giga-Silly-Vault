#Theory 
This is related to solar farming, the idea that us, as humans, are farming the energy of [[The Sun]], and we are farming it too much, and the net value of heat is dropping, which will eventually make the sun run out of heat, and eventually die. 

This can be interpreted as there being way too many sources of [[Fire]] nowadays, so the fire spirits are summoned more and more frequently, causing them to be more and more related to this world, causing the sun to grow weaker and weaker, cause we as [[Human]]s grow stronger and stronger. 

One day, we will outgrow the sun.
