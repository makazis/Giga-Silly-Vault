#Theory 
This is of course related to [[Hollow Earth]]. 
The tibetan holy books said that beneath mount everest there were these tunnel systems that spread all over the world. 
The majority of cave systems are completely unexplored. 

Most of these systems are closed up by [[The Government]], and in most cases it is a felony if you try to access them. This means that the government doesn't want us to get into the [[Hollow Earth]]. because obviously.

Also every single culture has a legend of creatures that come from underground. Also religions having the common idea of [[Demon]]s living underneath, and troglodytes living underground as well, and the idea that you can dig your way down into [[Hell]] (which of course is a psyops, because beneath us is [[Heaven]], obviously.) 
Underneath [[The Pyramids]] there is a massive underground cave system, and the structures in [[Antartica]] were a signal to get underground. Also some ancient societies just disappearing into caves all at once.
