# Explanation
Within abandoned buildings there are elevator shafts that go far in excess of the buildings dimensions. 
In some cases the elevator is permanently above the first floor, standing on the second or the third floor, and when they stand on the first floor, and shine a flashlight downwards, it seems like it goes on forever. 
There are rare maps of where these are located, and there are upwards of 500 of these endless shafts in existence. 

This is a transport system between the [[Human Realm]] and the [[Hollow Earth]]

The subterrarean creatures are using these elevators as a mean of getting above ground.

Whoever is building these buildings has either direct knowledge of them, or partial knowledge of these things. 

People who go missing in cities sometimes are just taken to the [[Hollow Earth]] in order to preform rituals and strengthen the subterrarean entities. 

Underground laboratories, and undegroud train systems.

There are several sites around the world where elevator shafts connect the above world into the world below. Through these the subterrarean creatures are able of dragging people into the hollow earth, making them [[Missing Person|missing people]].
