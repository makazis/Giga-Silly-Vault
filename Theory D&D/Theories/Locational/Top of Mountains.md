# Explanation

There is a cosmic sort of power or importance to [[Mountain]] tops. If there’s an unseen supernatural or [[Vibrations|vibrational]] world around us, mountains are the peak where they transition from our world into theirs. This is mostly due to the lack of interference of other vibrational forces that are at the lower points on earth. The construction of [[The Pyramids]] was also to simulate the top of a mountain, and be more powerful. 

Since [[Jesus]] ascended on the top of a [[Mountain]], it’s the gateway between this world and the metaphysical.

Some [[Cryptid]]s can use the gateway at the top of the mountains to traverse out of our field of comprehension.
