#Theory 
# Explanation
There is a realm of the unseen that we can’t interact with ([[Spiritual Plane]]), and all of the structures like the temples in [[The Pyramids]], caverns of [[Hollow Earth]], tops of [[Mountain]]s, temples in caves, etc,  are gateways to this realm. In these places you can cross over from this realm into the other realm, or communicate with the creatures in there. All of these things are subtly connected as a means of communicating between each other.
