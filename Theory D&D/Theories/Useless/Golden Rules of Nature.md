#Theory 
related to the five basic golden rules of nature. 

for something to survive in nature, it has to have balance, growth, connection, harmonization and love. sounds a bit weird, but let’s break it down a bit. 

You have to be balanced in the way that you live, because you can’t be too much one way or the other. This doesn’t apply to humans only, but things in nature as a whole.

You have to grow, if you’re stagnant for too long, you’ll die. 

You have to be connected to the world around you, because isolation also leads to death.

You have to harmonize with the world around you, because if you take too much, or are too violent, it will eventually come back.

And the love is saying that there has to be a passion or a drive, because depression is a killer. 

If the physical realm is the representative of the spiritual realm, then perhaps those five rules also apply to the spiritual world. If one is not balanced, growing, harmonized, connected and in love with their spiritual lifestyle, then that leads to a spiritual death. And since we aren’t following these five rules, the society as a whole is spiritually dying, and will lead to something bad in the future. because we do not treat spiritual existence the same way we treat physical existence.
