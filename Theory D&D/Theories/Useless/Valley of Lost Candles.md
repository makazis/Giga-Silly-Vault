#Theory 
Is in reference to stolen props from the 1967 movie "Valley Of The Dolls."
Because of the shady stuff that happens in holywood, the film was seen as a look behind the scenes of what happens in there. 
Several of the props like the candles used in the movie were used in actual [[Theory D&D/Concepts/Magic/Magic|witchcraft]] [[ritual]]s, and carried bad spirits with them. Those props were stolen immediately after the movie.
