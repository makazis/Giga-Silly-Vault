#Theory
an RPG called Hybrid, cancelled in 2002. 
The only existing text file was a ruleset that is roughly 30% completed, and is completely massive, and the rules are really weird, and cross reference each other. 
Several of the rules are just really long equations of how [[Gravity]] interacts in a sphere, and stuff like that. 
At one point the author begins to talk about [[The Simulation]], and how important it is to understand the walls of the simulation you're within. That goes back to the theory is that they tried to create a ruleset for the world we exist in. This RPG itself was a look through the cracks for what our reality actually is.
