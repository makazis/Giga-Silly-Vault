# Explanation

Long before time had a name, the humans were essentially the nervous system and the consciousness, and the physical parts and the [[Human Skin]] were a different entity, and together they made a symbiotic relationship to fuse into one entity. This is a process seen in the wild known as endosymbiosis, 

It means that [[Death]] is not a death of your mind, it is a separation of your consciousness from the [[Alien]] parasite taking over your skin.
