# Explanation
[[Theory D&D/Things/Materials/Smart Dust|Smart Dust]] is a powder, or a dust, that is made up of very microscopic [[nanomachines]], that whenever exposed to different environments like lights or temperature, they can move or interact with each other differently, forming different structures. 

The smart dust was indeed developed, and then put into [[Consumable Products|food and Water]] to make people into [[Sleeper Agent|sleeper agents]]. The dust cannot be detected on its own, but when activated, it can do anything, like move the [[Human|person]], causing changes in your mental state, and is the idea that because of this smart dust, everyone is a [[Sleeper Agent]].

# Interpretation
Boy, am I starting to feel sorry for the human condition. So now there’s small dust that can control your body from the inside when activated. Let’s say, the activation is different specific frequencies of invisible light. It can make people sad, make them aggressive, make them attack specific other people, etc. All you have to do to crack this is learn the frequency at which these effects transpire.

This makes smart dust a potential [[Cognitohazard]].
