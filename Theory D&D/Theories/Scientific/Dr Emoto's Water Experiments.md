# Explanation
[[Masaru Emoto]] was doing experiments with how [[Water]] interacted with different types of vibrations. [[Vibrations]] in the air and in the earth can be used to heal certain individuals and certain ailments. Different waters hold different personality characteristics. Water that came from mountains and springs froze differently than water that came from cities or drainages. Waters exposed to pleasant and unpleasant vibrations show different patterns. (This does include the way that you react around the water too, as well as the imagery around the water itself) After the water was frozen, it displayed different frozen characteristics. This implies that water is sentient. Water is the basis of existence and life. Water exists as a higher form of existence than humans can comprehend. Water is partially alive.
