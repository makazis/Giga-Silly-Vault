#Theory 
The way that [[The Sun]] heats up is really weird. There corona is the brim around the sun, that shines bright.

This can be explained like this: If you are standing near a campfire, and are feeling very very hot, and then take a step back from the campfire, you are now cool, because of the distance. 

This isn't how it works with the sun though, but the leading theory is that it's due to the magnetic field. The field can kinda move over to you if you got a magnetic field. How it reaches the earth is a bit of a mystery though. Around the sun the corona works as a sort of [[Aura]], that is used to give specific entities [[Heat]]. 

The sun is either a living entity, or whatever is controlling the corona around it is either an old [[God]], or a spirit, allowing our world to exist.
