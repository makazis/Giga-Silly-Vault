# Explanation
There was an idea between three letter agencies in the [[United States]] to make a sort of viewless recorder or in other words a camera that is not visible. It was called the glass camera because of this. A camera with no evidence of recording something. The details of how to make this camera were never made public. 

It wasn’t built using physical properties, but by using psychic properties. There’s a famed [[psychic]] by the name of [[Robert Golf]], which [[The Government]]  has done a good job at eliminating the record of his existence, who worked with the government on [[Theory D&D/Concepts/Things one can do/Astral Projection]]. 

[[Robert Golf]] would be hooked up to the imagers that attach to his head, and then his thoughts would be put on a screen. Then Robert would astrally project out of his body, and go view something as a ghost or a specter. That was later wired back to his brain, which was wired back to a television, and that’s how the glass camera works. 

One time Robert was out of his body for too long while astral projecting, and for a few brief seconds went to [[Heaven]], to which the image of heaven was transmitted to his brain, and on the TV, which means there is a video footage of two seconds of heaven somewhere, as immediately after, the footage was stolen by [[The Catholic Church]], and remains in the [[Vatican Vault]] to this day.

# Interpretation

Alright, many things.

First of all, there is a way to create a camera by hooking a [[Human]] that is astrally projecting out of their body, and the camera is undetectable. This means that while astrally projecting, your [[Soul]] leaves your body, and can’t be registered by common technology or the human eye. 

Second of all, if a person dies like this, you can get insight on what happens for a few seconds as they pass into the afterlife, meaning you can technically try to obtain footage of [[Hell]], or other realms on camera too. If you take a picture of the [[Hyper Reality]], it kills anyone who views the image, as it becomes a [[Cognitohazard]].

Third of all. There is found footage of heaven in the Vatican vault.
