#Theory 
# Explanation

[[Christian God]] created the heavens and the earth. The earth was without form and void. The question here is that if God created the [[Heaven]]s and the earth, and after that [[The Earth]] got developed, then what was the original earth?

Ties into the great reset, the idea that we, as a humanity, exist continuously on a cycle that occurs every so many million years, where we reach a breaking point, fall apart, and humanity is restarted.

# Interpretation

[[The God]] breaking down was the catalyst of the world being reborn, which means that the [[The Before|humans of the old world]] either achieved [[Enlightenment]], or succumbed to an end of the world. Either way, biblical explanation of [[The Great Cycle]], and the great reset theory.
