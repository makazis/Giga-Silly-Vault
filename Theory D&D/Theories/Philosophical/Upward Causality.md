#Theory 
Cause and effect, but backwards.

The animal that wants to eat another animal makes that other animal have the desire to eat food, which makes the food to have the desire to take in water, which makes the sky want to produce more water.

This is related to [[The Simulation]] theory. If you, as the person, need to intake food, the system puts more food into it, so it has to supply water for the food and etc, and etc. So we are beings that make the food appear, instead of the food being there is what lets us be alive.
