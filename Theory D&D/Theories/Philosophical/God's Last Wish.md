#Theory 
# Explanation
Almost across every religion, in the beginning, [[God]] created humanity in order to have a companion, But, it’s not really a companion if it’s a perfect copy, so [[The God]]’s last wish is that he broke himself into infinite pieces in order to create free will. The one thing being better than divinity was the existence of choice. In nearly every religion humanity separated themselves from god because of a selfish choice. [[Human]]s exist as pieces of god that have done whatever they wanted to with that free will.  Every single person is a piece of the perfect god. And they have been given the opportunity to use that piece of the perfect [[Soul]] in order to do whatever, and to make whatever choices they wish. That also means that humanity as a whole has a purpose, a demonstration of the concept of choice in order to eventually return to being perfect and divine.

# Interpretation
Humans all have a part of God's divine soul, which is why we have free will at all.
