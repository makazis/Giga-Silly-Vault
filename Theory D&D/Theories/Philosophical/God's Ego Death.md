The other definition of [[Ego Death]] is that during very very hardcore trips on [[Drugs]], your [[Soul]] leaves your body. Or when your soul escapes your mind, and your concept of thought and existence. It’s separating a part of yourself from another part of yourself. This is talking about [[The God]] separating from themselves, instead of having a change of heart. At one point god was just a single entity, but now it has separated into those many smaller gods, and many smaller entities, such as [[Human]]s. Perhaps the patterns and the fractals people see when they look into hyper reality, is that they see the pieces of the one true perfect god now broken down into these pieces of the former divinity.
