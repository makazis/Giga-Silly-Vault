# **Explanation:**
Whenever a gap in the human understanding is formed, that is where the christians fill it with a [[God]], and that is where [[The God]]
of the gaps comes from. Whenever humanity says there is a [[God]], that’s because people can’t explain something, and god makes for a good scapegoat.

A lazy writeoff for understanding, Henry said that whenever christians don’t understand something, they fill it with [[God]]. [[God]] has to be [[The God]] of everything, or he simply doesn’t exist. 

In other words, we can’t have god be this spontaneous miracle worker, that shows up to fill things we can’t quite explain, and then fill the rest away as natural processes of science. Either [[God]] is absolute, or [[God]] doesn’t exist. 

This is the reason that the belief in christianity has declined so much in the past century, Before modern science there wasn’t a way to explain the world outside of some supernatural entity. Since then, gaps have gotten smaller, and that has made [[God]] smaller. 

If there is a [[God]], it has to be an absolute [[God]].
# **Interpretation:**
There’s one super [[God]] that exists above all other regular, conceptual gods, that is the embodiment of EVERYTHING. (if there wasn’t there couldn’t be a [[God]], which disproves the existence of all other gods)
