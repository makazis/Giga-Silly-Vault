
#Cult 
# Things to note:

- this is less of a cult, more of a collective consciousness that controls the [[Soulless]] people. They have no free will, no free thought, and yet, they have some ability to work together. 
    
- This means that this is one of the cults that decides whether or not the people get to go to [[Heaven]] or [[Hell]]. 
    
- They are immune to any and all mental attacks, and manipulations due to having no [[Soul]]. Once one is attacked this way, all others in the area will go to protect the one, turning a friend into a foe.
