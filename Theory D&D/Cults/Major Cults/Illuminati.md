#Cult 

# Things to note:

- There is a teaching within the illuminati, that teaches the user about a specific pattern, used in the incorruptible [[Saint]]s, that allows them to become one after [[Death]]. After that, they forge a deal, that their [[Soul]] will join an universal veil between the illuminati and the outside world, storing their memories, and upkeeping knowledge, as well as giving the cult members access to [[Magic]]. This also turns some of the cult members into [[Pattern Manipulator]]s. 
    
- The Illuminati are one of the groups that get to decide whether or not a [[Soul]] can ascend to [[Heaven]] or to go to [[Hell]].  

The elder members of the [[Illuminati]] are just souls [[Possession|Possesing]] mortals, in order to achieve [[Immortallity]], which means they have to consume some people each year, to take their bodies, which explains some of the [[Missing Person]] cases around the world. 

The [[Illuminati]] take these drastic measures in order to protect the world, as they are actually the good guys, as they are fighting against [[The Government]], and keeping them in check. 

Also the [[Illuminati]] is founded in [[France]].
