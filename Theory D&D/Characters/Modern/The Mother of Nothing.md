#Character 
One of the Mothers of [[The Family Buisness]].

# Powers
The Mother of Nothing has the inherent ability of concept manipulation. She can force a concept into life by breathing enough [[Soul]] into it. Soon after it becomes real, it will begin to deteriorate, as it will have an existential crisis, if it can think, and will most likely become yet another [[Hyper Beast]]. 

She can create the concept of infinite distance between herself and an object she can see, meaning that object can never reach her. This means that she can not be touched by anything she can see. 
She can also manifest anything into existance, at any point. The only two limits are her soul HP, and her imagination.
