Lots of tatoos, all over the body. Several scars too. Black, long hair, usually wearing more than three necklaces, body covered in tattoos, earrings. She loves to customize herself.


Powers:
- Ruth has the ability to visualise and manipulate [[Concept]]s in the [[Conceptual Plane]], as long as she draws a rune on the [[Body]] of the concept in the [[Human Realm]], creating specific areas in space where they begin to manifest. This means that she can push the concept of a wall outside of the wall itself, so that the wall begins existing only if viewed from one side, letting people to pass through from the other. This also allows to push the concepts of entities out of other themselves, either ejecting a soul, or making a person unresponsive for a short while. Since she does need to draw some runes onto the thing she is trying to manipulate, it takes time, and a bit of effort, but the stuff she can do with her powers is truly immersive
	- Ruth can push the concept of a person away from themselves, meaning that they cease to exist unless they are viewed by her directly. Meaning that all she has to do is close her eyes, and the person stops existing as a person, and becomes a concept, as if under a [[Store]] spell. 
Weaknesses:
- Ruth has to draw a complicated rune on the thing she is trying to control. this usually takes between 30 seconds and a minute, but can take longer. Also if she messes up the rune, it can backfire, exploding in her face, or with other major defects. 
- Ruth can only draw up to two runes at a time
- The runes she draws disappear after she falls unconscious
- Ruth cannot draw runes on herself, as she will not be able to bring herself out from non-existence
