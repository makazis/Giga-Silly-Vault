#Thing 
A group of [[Tree]]s. 
Has a [[Hivemind]], and stores some [[Theory D&D/Concepts/Magic/Magic]]al energy and [[Mana]] in them because of that. [[Human]]s staying here for extended periods of time begin to [[Decalcification|Decalcify]] their body, and become more susceptible to the [[Spiritual Plane]], letting them attain the ability to use magic.
