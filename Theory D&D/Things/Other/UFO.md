#Thing 
An UFO is an unidentified flying object. Often connected with [[Alien]].
There are three types of UFO's:
-  Nano-UFO: Small enough to enter a [[Human]]'s bloodstream, and turn them into a [[Sleeper Agent]]. 
- Giga-UFO: Large enough to be clouds, [[Star]]s, or the entire [[Sky]] itself. These mostly exist for [[Surveilance System|surveilance]] reasons, because we exist in [[The Simulation|A Simulation]].
