#Disease #Thing 
A lethal disease, that has the following symptoms:
- Extreme aversion to [[Water]].
- Confusion
- Fever
- Excessive Salivary Secretion
- Hallucinations
- Disrupted [[Sleep]].
- Paralysis
- Coma
- Hyperactivity
- Headache
- Nausea
- Vomiting
- Anxiety.
It has a [[Vaccine]] that treats it.
