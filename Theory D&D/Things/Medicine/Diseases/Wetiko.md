#Thing #Disease
A native [[United States|American]] legend, that has to do with the spirit of greed and selfishness.
It is a virus that people can get infected with, that makes you think of yourself, instead of a community. 
The root word for Wetiko is the same as the root word for [[Wendigo]], but instead of one becoming [[Cannibal|cannibalistic]], and [[Gluttony]] overtaking one, the desire for [[Pride]] and [[Greed]] overtakes them. 
This disease of the Wetiko has affected entire countries or the world at this point. The world looks less at the progression of the world, and more at the progression of self. 

Somehow related to [[Soy]] too if you think about it.
