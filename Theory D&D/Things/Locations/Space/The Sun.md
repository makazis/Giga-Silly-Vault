#Location 
Very hot ball of fire in the space. 
It brings heat to [[The Earth]]
The sun is a [[God]], a greater one at that. 
Inside of the sun, is where [[Hell]] exists. Or, a subregion of hell.
Since a [[Soul]] can be transformed into [[Mana]], all the souls that go to hell sometimes arrive at the sun, where they are eternally tortured, by draining their life force over and over again, so the sun can burn with eternal [[Fire]].
