#Concept 
By ingesting another thing, and vomiting it back out, you can cleanse your [[Human]] body both in the spiritual and in the physical sense. 
The type of cleaning depends on the essence of the thing that you swallow, and the strongest essence of them all is a [[Soul]], so humans, and especially humans with [[Theory D&D/Concepts/Magic/Magic]]al capabilities are more potent in these sorts of rituals.

This does mean that in order to consume a [[Human]], you must become a [[Cannibal]]. This is a common practice by the world [[Elitist]]s.
