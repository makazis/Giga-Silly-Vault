#Concept #Action
If a [[Human]] [[Cannibal|eats a]] [[Body]] of a [[Corpse]], they gain the ability to gain the remnants of their [[Soul]], which can be turned into either [[Mana]] or additional lifespan. By doing this enough, one can achieve [[Immortallity]]. this is how [[Vampire]]s work, they eat the corpses of dead people to become immortal. 
Some of the worlds [[Elitist]]s as well as several cults, such as [[The Government]], or [[Illuminati]] have been practicing this in order to stay immortal. 

Do this enough, and you become a powerful [[Theory D&D/Concepts/Magic/Magic]] wielder.
