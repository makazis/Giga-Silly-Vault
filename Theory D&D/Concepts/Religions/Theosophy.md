#Concept #Religion
The belief that there are Masters in this world, mostly around Tibet, who have gathered great wisdom and supernatural powers. They are trying to revive knowledge of an ancient religion once found around the world that will again come to eclipse existing world religions. 

Also the religion that there is one hidden truth in the world, which if you learn, you can begin manipulating with. And there are some reality hackers who have figured out what the one truth is, which are known as the Masters. They understand how [[The Universe]] works. They can perform [[Theory D&D/Concepts/Magic/Magic]], and witchcraft, because they understand how the world works better than we do.
