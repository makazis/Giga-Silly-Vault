#Concept 
Timeline Hopping is when you jump from one [[Time]]line into another. This usually happens at the corners of time, as explained in [[Timecube]], 

There are two main ways to perform timeline hopping:
- The Doppelganger Method:
	- By using a [[Ritual]], or some sort of [[Theory D&D/Concepts/Magic/Magic]], or interplanar travel, you can jump into another timeline, but this usually results in an [[Angel]] tracking you down, and returning you to your own timeline, but taking your [[Soul]] as punishment for trying to cheat the system. 
- The Swap Method: 
	- By aligning yourself with the other you in the same place, at the same time, you can swap your consciousness with the other individual. This is the more safer option, as this can't be traced by [[Angel]]s. This is incredibly hard to pull of though.
