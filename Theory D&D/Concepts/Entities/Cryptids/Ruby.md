#Cryptid 
Ruby is a creature that was born within [[The Moon]].

Appearance:
- Ruby usually takes the appearance of a red, light refracting crystal sphere. When Ruby is observed by a creature, Ruby takes the appearance of whatever the creature wills for Ruby to exist as. 

Powers:
- Ruby can gain any power and appearance that the creature observing it believes Ruby should have. If that person believes Ruby should fall, Ruby will begin falling. If they think Ruby can float, Ruby will float. If they think they are facing a dragon, Ruby will become a dragon. If they think that they can hear their dead son, Ruby will turn into their dead son.
	- Ruby will not show any abilities that the person observing it does not believe it should have. 
		- If the observer believes they are talking to their dead son, and does not believe that their dead son is alive, they will remain on the ground, "deceased".
		- If the observer believes they are fighting a dragon, but does not know that a dragon spits fire, then Ruby will not spit fire. 
- If the observer can imagine multiple things at the same time, Ruby can choose which form to manifest as. 
Things to note:
- Ruby will latch onto the first creature that sees it, and will change it's loyality to another when it believes its current observer is gone. 

Ruby still remains inside of the moon, and will as long as there are no creatures going in or out of the moon.
