# Things to note:

- Saints are like the greatest people to ever exist. [[God]] created and what not. 
    
- The [[Soul]] of a saint tends to linger in their [[Corpse]] after their death, meaning that their [[Corpse]] is way more powerful than other [[Corpse]]s. 
    
- The people with the ability to become incorruptible saints, that haven’t been recruited by cults, are usually hunted down, in order for the souls in their bodies to be harvested, to make the cults stronger themselves. 
    
- There are cults that make deals with people before they die, where they put an end to their souls' immortality after some years, and while the soul is alive, it protects the cult from other phenomena, as a shield of a soul.
