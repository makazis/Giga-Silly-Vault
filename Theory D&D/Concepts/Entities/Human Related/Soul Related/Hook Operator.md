#Concept #Human #Entity 
A [[Hook Operator]] is able to make other [[Human]]s see the world as they see it, by spreading a part of their charisma or their essence.
Basically it means that [[Soulless]] people can be partially controlled by [[Hook Operator]]s, as well as some weaker people with [[Soul]]s. It also means that these people can become lesser [[Hivemind]]s, by adopting masses upon masses of human population. 

Some of the worlds [[Elitist]]s and [[Politician]]s, as well as members of larger cults such as the [[Illuminati]] or [[The Government]] are [[Hook Operator]]s.
