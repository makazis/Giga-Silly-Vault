There are three local wizards in a coastal city that are trying to repair their connection to the main hub. They are pretty chill about it. 
There are three wizards, each with different personalities, and they have hired the party to defend the portal, in case something bad comes out.

The wizards show the party some routes that they can get through that they will need to do in order to fix their portal

A map of these routes can be seen at [[The Map]]

Some ideas for what's happening along the way
Some random events that can happen on each spot of the map too maybe. Nah, let's have them all pre-planned

- Combat Encounters:
	- # The Angry Gorillas:
		- The party is taken to a primordial jungle where they are surrounded by a bunch of monkey like creatures, that attack the party. They can be warded off. This can lead to [[#The Happy Gorillas]]
	- # Passage Below:
		- This is an one way portal into the hell dimension, causing for the hell path to be entered, which the party must go through to get back on track, or die trying, remaining in hell eternally. 
- Roleplay Encounters:
	- # Crystal Broke:
		- During the replacement of the crystal, it breaks, and must be replaced. The crystal breaking creates a nuclear level of explosion, causing for the wizards to sit inside of a protective bubble with a deck of cards. 
	- # The Happy Gorillas:
		- Very similar to [[#The Angry Gorillas]]. There is a group of semi sentient, tool using gorillas that approach the party, and try to exchange items. this will ALWAYS lead to the angry gorillas, as they are one dimension apart.
