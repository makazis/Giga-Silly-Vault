	- Verbal:
		- (3.1) "Flectere si nequeo superos, Acheronta movebo." (If I can not bend the will of Heaven, I shall move Hell)
	- Somatic:
		- (2) The following hand sign must be made with the two real hands, while the third hand levitates above them.
		- ![[Pasted image 20260402163837.png|188]]
- Effect:
	- Instead of a shower of sparks coming down from the sky above the target, from the earth a fiery trident emerges to about eye height, and explodes. This is when the dex save is rolled, and everyone takes damage.
