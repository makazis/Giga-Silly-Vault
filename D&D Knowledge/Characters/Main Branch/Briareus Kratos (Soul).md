Brother of [[Grunken Kratos]] and [[Gyges Kratos]] 
Reborn as a son of [[Tex Dinoco]] and [[Lynda Dinoco]]. 

His new mother, Lynda, taught him how to access the magical domain known as [[Varkus]], allowing him to use the abilities that were his new bodies divine right. 

A couple hundred years down the line, these abilities were passed down, leaving the legacy of Briareus to a far descendant, who had half divine abilities themselves, [[Saryan Thredonne]].
