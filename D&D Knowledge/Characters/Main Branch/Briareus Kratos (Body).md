#Character 
The newborn that was reborn into the previous body of [[Briareus Kratos (Soul)]]. 


Backstory:


[[Briareus Kratos (Soul)|Briares]] fell in action, and his corpse was discovered 4 days later by a passing wizard. The wizard had been experimenting with the arcane, so he cast a modified [[Awaken]] spell on Briares, causing his dead body to regain consciousness. Unfortunately for the both of them, the creature, now sitting in front of the wizard was Briares in body, but not in spirit, as the body had been inhabited by a new soul. A soul without the fighting expertise of Briares, and a soul without the knowledge of the warrior that he was. After the wizard was done marvelling the spell, and had gotten some manual labor from the new Briares (he needed someone to fix his broken shed, as the ceiling had fallen down, and wouldn’t stay up with magic), Briares returned back to the village, where, immediately upon his return, he got congratulated on his success, and instructed on a great opportunity for an adventure. Still quite confused, and seeing all the happiness that creatures all around him saw about him going on the quest, he agreed, got into a carriage, and arrived here, where the questing shall begin.
