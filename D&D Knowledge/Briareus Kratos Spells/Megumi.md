
- Mechanical Description:
	- Guidance
	
	Source: Player's Handbook
	
	_Divination cantrip_
	
	**Casting Time:** 1 action  
	**Range:** Touch  
	**Components:** V, S  
	**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
	
	You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to one ability check of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the ability check. The spell then ends.
- Components:
	- Ancillary:
		- (3) [[Varkus]] is cast to make the eyes of the caster black, with small yellow pupils, with a lighter orange ring around the smaller yellow pupil. 
	- Somatic:
		- (1) The hands must be put together in a prayer, and the eyes of the caster must be closed. 
		- (4) The first person on who the eyes look upon gets the effect of the spell. The eyes remain in such a color as long as guidance is active. 
	- Verbal:
		- (2) "Aequam servare mentem"
